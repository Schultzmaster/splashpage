The Origin of Fakeblog

Designed from the get go with a special kind of user in mind, fakeblog is here for you anytime you need it. Your imaginary concept for what it is is just as valid as what it actually is. Bet you had to read that sentence twice. A website with no real content, just begging to be filled with your vapid expectaions.

Wait, isn't this a PureCSS layout?

Yep! And I freely admit it. I used it mostly for the side bar, but once I learned their class system I modified them for my purposes. So it's a blend of the default settings and my personal adaptations. While I may not have come up with all the code, I do understand all of it, and how to use it. Ok, I understand most of it, but no less than I did the photography website.
